About Speeko Buddy

SpeekoBuddy is a great tool to write fast. It can help you when you are in hurry and do not want to use your pretty hands! SpeekoBuddy has a lot more features:

  • SpeekoBuddy simply says - "You speek, I Write"

  • It can easily run anywhere.
  • Saves a lot of your precious time.

Guide To Use Speeko Buddy

Although it is very easy to use. But Let me show you the proper guide to demonstrate how to use it.

  • Step 1: Click on Start Recognition to write what you speak. It will ask for microphone permission that should be allowed in order to use the functionality
  • Step 2: Click on Stop Recognition to stop the Recognition.
  • Step 3: After the completion of speech you can save by clicking on save note. These notes will be shown below
